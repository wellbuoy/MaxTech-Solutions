Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and products.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements or software issues are completed within 2-4 hours. More complex hardware repairs may take 24-48 hours. We offer same-day service for many repairs when you bring your device in before noon.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 30-day warranty on parts and labor. For certain repairs like screen replacements, we offer extended warranty options.

Can you recover data from damaged devices?

In most cases, yes. We have specialized tools and techniques to recover data from devices with physical damage, water damage, or software corruption. Success rates vary by situation, but we'll always give you an honest assessment before proceeding.

Do you buy used devices?

Yes, we purchase used smartphones, tablets, and laptops in good condition. Bring your device in for a free evaluation and competitive offer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major cash send, cash, credit cards and debit cards . Financing options are available for purchases over R2,000.
